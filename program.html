Program Descriptions

Mahjong (Free)

Join our friendly mahjong group every Sunday afternoon. All skill levels are welcome. Enjoy games, make new friends, and relax in a welcoming environment.

Chinese Dance

Experience the beauty of traditional Chinese dance. Our classes are open to all ages and skill levels, focusing on movement, music, and cultural expression.

Tai Ji Class

Practice Tai Ji to improve balance, flexibility, and overall wellness. Our weekly sessions are led by experienced instructors and suitable for beginners and advanced students.

Family Support

Receive guidance and resources for families adjusting to life in Canada. We offer support groups, counseling, and information sessions to help you thrive.

Chinese Painting

Discover the art of Chinese painting. Learn brush techniques, color mixing, and traditional styles in a relaxed and creative atmosphere.

Computer Class

Build your digital skills with our computer classes. Topics include basic computer use, internet safety, and popular software applications. Open to all ages.

Yoga Group

Relax and rejuvenate with our yoga group. Sessions focus on gentle stretching, breathing exercises, and mindfulness for all experience levels.

Community Event

Participate in special events and workshops throughout the year. These gatherings are designed to build community, share knowledge, and celebrate cultural traditions.
