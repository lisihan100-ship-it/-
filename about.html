About Us

Vision

Community Family Services of Ontario is recognized as the premier provider of family services to support our community, where all families live in a safe and harmonious environment.

Mission

We help build a safe and harmonious community by providing professional counselling, settlement, disabilities, and special needs services to individuals and families in need.

2020s

Continued growth in programs for newcomers, youth, seniors, and multi-ethnic communities, reinforcing CAM as a welcoming hub for immigrants in the Greater Toronto Area.

Expanded cultural events and community programs, including New Year celebrations, Mid-Autumn Festival, Hong Kong Festival, Rainbow Festival, and Dragon Boat races.
