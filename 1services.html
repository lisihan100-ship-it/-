Our Services

We provide comprehensive, FREE services for Permanent Residents and Convention Refugees/Protected Person.

Settlement Services

Complete support for your Canadian settlement journey.

  • PR card renewal assistance
    We help prepare documents and provide guidance for PR card renewal.
  • Information and referral services
    Connect to local resources, government programs and partner agencies.
  • Government document support
    Assistance with filling out and submitting various government forms.
Learn more

Language & Training

Free English instruction with comprehensive support.

  • LINC classes (Levels 1–5)
    Language Instruction for Newcomers to Canada from basic to advanced.
Learn more

Community Services

Building connections and providing ongoing support.

  • Community events and activities
    Participate in events that connect and engage residents.
  • Cultural integration programs
    Programs to help newcomers integrate into Canadian society.
  • Family support services
    Counselling, resources and referrals for families.
  • Free childcare support
    Childcare available for students attending our classes.
  • Free bus tickets for qualified students
    Eligible students can receive bus tickets for transportation.
Learn more

Eligibility Requirements

Valid PR Status:

Must have valid PR Card or PR confirmation letter (landing paper)

Convention Refugees:

Protected person with Notice of Decision

Hours of Operations

Mon To Fri: 9 am – 5 pm
