Our Services

We provide comprehensive, FREE services for Canadian immigrants and refugees with permanent residence cards

Settlement Services

Complete support for your Canadian settlement journey

  • PR card renewal assistance
    We help you renew your Permanent Resident card, including document preparation and submission guidance.
  • Information and referral services
    Get connected to local resources, government programs, and community organizations.
  • Government document support
    Assistance with filling out and submitting government forms and applications.

Language & Training

Free English language instruction with comprehensive support

  • LINC classes (Levels 1-5)
    Language Instruction for Newcomers to Canada (LINC) classes are available for all levels, from basic to advanced.
  • Free childcare support
    We offer free childcare support for children of students attending our language classes.
  • Free bus tickets for qualified students
    Eligible students can receive free bus tickets to assist with transportation to and from classes.

Community Services

Building connections and providing ongoing community support

  • Community events and activities
    Join us for various community events and activities designed to connect and engage residents.
  • Cultural integration programs
    Programs to help newcomers integrate into Canadian society and culture.
  • Family support services
    Support services for families, including counseling, resources, and referrals.

Eligibility Requirements

Valid PR Status:
Must have valid PR Card or PR confirmation letter (landing paper)
Canadian Resident:
Be a Canadian immigrant or refugee with permanent residence
