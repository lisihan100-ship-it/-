Carassauga China Pavilion 2026

Carassauga Festival will be held on May 2026. Hosted by the City of Mississauga, it celebrates Canada’s multiculturalism with food, music, dance and cultural performances from over twenty countries.

About the Pavilion

The Chinese Association of Mississauga (CAM) is the host of the China Pavilion and has long promoted Chinese culture, showcasing both traditional heritage and modern civilization. Visitors can explore booths, taste authentic cuisine, and enjoy music & dance performances.

Interactive activities include calligraphy and paper-folding demonstrations for all ages.
