Volunteer with CAM

Why Volunteer

Join us as a volunteer and be part of a dynamic organization that promotes Chinese culture, fosters community, and creates lasting memories!

Volunteering at the Chinese Association of Mississauga (CAM) is a rewarding experience that offers both personal and professional growth.

Contact Us to Volunteer High school students can earn OSSD hours

Volunteer Benefits

  • Gain Experience: Connect with a diverse community and build meaningful relationships.
  • Expand Your Network: Attend cultural festivals, members-only workshops, and networking events.
  • Make a Positive Impact: Support Chinese heritage, language, and traditions in Mississauga and beyond.
  • Personal Growth: Participate in community service and charitable initiatives.
  • Recognition & Perks: Receive certificates, letters of recommendation, and exclusive event access.
  • Earn Community Service Hours: High school students can fulfill OSSD volunteer requirements while gaining experience.
