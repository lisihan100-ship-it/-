Make a Donation

The Chinese Association of Mississauga (CAM) is a non-profit organization dedicated to preserving Chinese culture, supporting the community, and fostering cultural exchange. Your donation helps us continue our mission and expand our programs.

Donate in Person

You can make a donation in person at our office during regular business hours. We accept cash, debit, and credit card payments.

Office Location

1177 Central Pkwy W Unit 16, Mississauga, ON L5C 4P3

Hours of Operation

Mon To Fri: 9 am – 5 pm

For large donations or special arrangements, please contact (905) 275-8558 or info@chineseassociationmississauga.com in advance.

Donate by Cheque or Money Order

Please make your cheque or money order payable to "Chinese Association of Mississauga Inc." and mail or deliver it to our office.

Address

Chinese Association of Mississauga, 1177 Central Pkwy W Unit 16, Mississauga, ON L5C 4P3

Important Information
  • Include your full name, address, and telephone number.
  • Indicate if you would like a tax receipt.
  • Specify any particular program you wish to support (optional).

Tax receipts will be issued for donations of $20 or more.

How Your Donation Helps

  • Cultural Events & Festivals: Carassauga China Pavilion, Asian Night Market, traditional celebrations.
  • Community Services: Workshops, language programs, seniors & youth support.
  • Volunteer & Leadership: Opportunities for youth leadership and engagement.
  • Charitable Efforts: Local/international relief and community aid.
