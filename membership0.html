Membership Registration

Chinese Association of Mississauga (CAM) Membership Registration

To become a member of CAM, please complete the online Membership Registration Form and submit the membership fee payment at our CAM office. We will contact you in a timely manner once you submit the online membership application.

Apply Online Pay at office

Membership Terms and Conditions

Membership Rules

  • The association's membership is on an annual basis, and the membership expires on December 31 of each year.
  • Once you become a member, the membership fee is non-refundable.
  • When a member renews, regardless of the renewal date, the expiration date will be December 31 of the same year.
  • To become a member you must provide valid ID for the association to copy, such as driver's license, PR card, and passport.
  • Adult membership fee is CAD$30, and seniors 65 and older are CAD$25.
  • If your membership card is lost or damaged, there is a CAD$5 fee for each replacement.
  • Members must present their membership cards when participating in any association activities or using association services.
  • Members participating in any association activities must comply with the relevant regulations of each activity.
  • If a member engages in any inappropriate behavior or violates the law, the association has the right to expel the member and reserves the right to pursue legal action.

Confidentiality Clause

In accordance with Canadian Law regarding confidentiality of personal data, the Chinese Association of Mississauga will only use the member's personal data for internal purposes and will not disclose any personal information to any third-party organizations or association personnel.

Fees & ID

  • Adult: CAD$30 / year
  • Senior (65+): CAD$25 / year
  • Card replacement: CAD$5
  • Valid ID required: driver’s license / PR card / passport
  • All memberships expire on Dec 31

Member Benefits

  • Members can get up to one year of free membership when they join the first time.
  • Members can receive any services provided by the office free of charge (services may require prior appointment).
  • Members can attend events organized by the association for free or at a member discount.
