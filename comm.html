Community Services

We offer a variety of community services to support seniors, families, and students in Mississauga. Our programs are designed to help you connect with the community, access essential resources, and enhance your quality of life.

Community Connections

  • Volunteer Training
  • Line Dance
  • Chinese Painting
  • Taiji Class
  • Chinese Folk-Dance
  • PeiJing Opera
  • Choir Class
  • Chinese Calligraphy Class

Other Community Services

Community Volunteer Income Tax Program

Free tax clinic for low-income families provided by trained volunteers.

We can help you complete and file your income tax returns accurately and on time.

Season: February to April each year

Free Bus Tickets for Students

FREE BUS TICKETS for eligible students to help with transportation needs.

Contact us to find out if you qualify and how to apply for this program.

Free Childcare for Students

FREE CHILDCARE for eligible students who need supervision for their children while attending classes or studying.

Our qualified caregivers provide a safe and nurturing environment for children.

Hours of Operations

Mon To Fri: 9 am – 5 pm
