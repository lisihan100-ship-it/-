Make a Donation

The Chinese Association of Mississauga (CAM) is a non-profit organization dedicated to preserving Chinese culture, supporting the community, and fostering cultural exchange. Your donation helps us continue our mission and expand our programs.

Donate in Person

You can make a donation in person at our office during regular business hours. We accept cash, debit, and credit card payments.

Office Location

Mississauga, ON L5C 4P3

Hours of Operation

Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

If you are making a large donation or need to arrange a special time, please contact us in advance at (905) 275-8558 or info@chineseassociationmississauga.com.

Donate by Cheque or Money Order

Please make your cheque or money order payable to "Chinese Association of Mississauga" and mail it to our office address.

  • Indicate if you would like a tax receipt

How Your Donation Helps

Your generous contribution supports a variety of important initiatives:

  • Cultural Events & Festivals: Organizing community events like the Carassauga China Pavilion, Mississauga Asian Night Market, and traditional Chinese celebrations.
  • Community Services: Funding educational workshops, language programs, senior support services, and youth development initiatives.
  • Volunteer & Leadership Programs: Providing opportunities for young leaders to develop skills through community engagement.
  • Charitable & Humanitarian Efforts: Assisting with local and international relief efforts and community aid programs.

Donation Recognition & Benefits

  • Official tax receipt for all eligible donations
  • Invitations to exclusive donor appreciation events
  • Public acknowledgment on our website, newsletters, and event materials (optional)
