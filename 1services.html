LINC English Classes & Free Childcare

Program Overview

LINC (Language Instruction for Newcomers to Canada) is funded by IRCC, and is a free language training program for all newcomers to Canada. We offer training for basic language skills to improve your English.

We provide Level 0 ~ 5 classes, as well as free daycare services for students who have children aged 1 years to 6-years old. We also provide free bus tickets for qualified students.

At a Glance

  • Levels: 0 – 5
  • Supportive Childcare: Free daycare for ages 1 years to 6 years old
  • Transportation: Free bus tickets for qualified students
  • Fee: FREE
