About Chinese Association of Mississauga (CAM)

The Chinese Association of Mississauga (CAM) is a non-profit organization and a registered charity organization. For over four decades, the CAM has been dedicated to supporting thousands of individuals and families in the community each year. Through a wide range of programs and services, CAM helps newcomers, seniors, youth, and families build connections, access resources, and thrive in the City of Mississauga and surrounding areas. While CAM receives funding from various government bodies, foundations, and community partners, your generous donations play a vital role in sustaining our efforts to serve and empower the community‚Äîhelping people create a better life in their new home.

Our Vision and Mission

The Chinese Association of Mississauga (CAM) is a non-profit organization and a registered charity organization. With over 40 years of record of experience serving the local communities, CAM has always been dedicated to promoting Chinese culture and showcasing both traditional and modern Chinese civilization.
